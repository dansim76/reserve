What we do

We connect retailers with charitable organizations to ensure that edible food never goes to waste.

weServe

Our goal is to facilitate an easy connection between our donors and those in need. We want grocery stores to be able to easily advertise food they have available to donate and for charitable organizations to be able to find what they need and get it quickly.

conServe

We also hope to make a considerable impact on the demand food production places on energy, land and fresh water resources.
